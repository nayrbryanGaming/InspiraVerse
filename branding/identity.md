# InspiraVerse Branding Identity

## 1. Logo Concept
**Minimalist Infinite Speech Bubble with Spark Icon**
- **Meaning**: Infinite inspiration, voice of wisdom, continuous growth.

## 2. Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#6C63FF` (Deep Electric Violet)
- **Secondary**: `#FFB84D` (Sunburst Orange)
- **Background**: `#F9FAFB` (Snow White)
- **Accent (Aura)**: Gradient of Violet to Fuchsia.

## 3. Typography
- **Primary (Headings)**: Outfit (Bold/Black)
- **Secondary (Body)**: Inter or Outfit (Medium)

## 4. AI Logo Prompt
> A minimalist vector logo for a mobile app named 'InspiraVerse'. The logo features a clean, stylized speech bubble forming an infinity symbol. Inside the bubble, a small, elegant spark icon glows in vibrant violet (#6C63FF). High contrast, white background, modern flat design, startup aesthetic, 4k resolution.

## 5. Visual Language
- **Gradients**: Subtle "Aura" gradients for headers.
- **Iconography**: Rounded, friendly, but professional (Fluent or Remix style).
- **Transitions**: Smooth, intentional micro-animations using `flutter_animate`.
