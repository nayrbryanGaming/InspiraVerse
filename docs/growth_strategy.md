# InspiraVerse Growth & KPI Strategy

## 1. Initial Target: 10,000 Installs
- **Timeline**: Q2 2026
- **Focus**: User acquisition and early retention benchmarking.

## 2. Growth Channels
- **TikTok/Shorts**: Daily motivational clips generated from the Designer Studio.
- **Instagram**: "Quote of the Day" aesthetic sharing.
- **Productivity Influencers**: Partnering with minimalist/biohacking creators.

## 3. Retention Strategy
- **Daily Notifications**: Precision-timed sparks of wisdom.
- **Streak System**: Gamified mental resilience building.
- **Personalized Curation**: Recommendation engine based on mood/category interaction.

## 4. Monetization Model
**Freemium (SaaS Model)**
- **Free**: Daily quote, basic designer, global feed.
- **Premium**:
  - Exclusive curated quote packs (Stoicism, High Performance).
  - Premium Designer textures and fonts.
  - Advanced resilience analytics.
  - Ad-free experience.
