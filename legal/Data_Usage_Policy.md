# Data Usage Policy for InspiraVerse

**Last Updated: April 23, 2026**

This policy provides transparency regarding how InspiraVerse collects and uses data to satisfy the Google Play Data Safety requirements.

## 1. Data Collected

### 1.1 Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Used for account authentication and service-related communications.
- **User ID**: A unique identifier generated by Firebase to link your data across devices.

### 1.2 App Activity
- **Quote Interactions**: We track which quotes you favorite or share to improve our recommendation engine (popularity score).
- **Journal Entries**: Stored securely to provide the journaling feature.

### 1.3 Device Information
- **Device IDs**: Used for Firebase Cloud Messaging (notifications).

## 2. Data Usage Purposes
- **App Functionality**: To provide the core features of the app (syncing, favorites, journaling).
- **Analytics**: To understand app usage patterns and improve user experience via Firebase Analytics.
- **Developer Communications**: To send important updates regarding the service.

## 3. Data Sharing
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not share your personal data with any third-party advertisers or data brokers.
- **Service Providers**: Data is processed by Google Firebase (our infrastructure provider) in accordance with their privacy standards.

## 4. Security & Retention
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Data is retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is purged within 30 days.
