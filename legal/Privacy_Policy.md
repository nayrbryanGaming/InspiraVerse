# Privacy Policy for InspiraVerse

**Last Updated: April 23, 2026**

InspiraVerse ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by InspiraVerse.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### 1.1 Personal Data
While using our Application, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information may include, but is not limited to:
- Email address
- First name and last name
- Usage Data

### 1.2 Usage Data
Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Application. This may include information such as your Device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data.

## 2. Data Safety & Sovereignty

We implement industry-standard encryption (AES-256 equivalent) for all data stored in the cloud. Your journal entries and favorites are private to you.

## 3. Google Play Compliance: Account Deletion

In compliance with Google Play policies, we provide a clear and easy way for you to delete your account and all associated data. You can find the "Delete Account" option in the Profile Settings within the Application. Upon request, all your data (including journals, favorites, and activity logs) will be permanently purged from our production systems.

## 4. No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with third parties.
